Our Story

Darasi was born from a passion for creating beautiful, minimalist jewelry that makes a statement without overwhelming. Each piece is handcrafted with care, using high-quality resin and carefully selected inclusions.

Our founder, a self-taught resin artist, started making jewelry as a hobby that quickly turned into a thriving business. Every Darasi piece reflects our core philosophy: luxury doesn't have to be complicated.
